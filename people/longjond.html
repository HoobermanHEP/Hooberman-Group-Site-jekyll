Jonathan Long




About me

Dr. Long works on the ATLAS experiment at the Large Hadron Collider (LHC). He has worked on measuring properties of the Higgs boson using the decay to two W bosons. Currently he is interested in searching for new physics, utilizing the higher-energy collisions of the LHC's second data-taking run. Dr. Long received his Ph.D and bachelor's degree from the University of Michigan (2015, 2010).
