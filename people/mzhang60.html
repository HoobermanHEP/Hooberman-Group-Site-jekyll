About me

Hello internet, my name is Matt Zhang and I'm in my first year of working with Ben and all the wonderful folks you saw on the last page. I've wanted to be a scientist ever since childhood, when I binge-watched a ton of NOVA episodes on PBS with my dad. I think the future is going to be an incredible, unrecognizable place, and that the science and technology to power it is partly going to come from the work that we do here. I'm currently at CERN doing searches for supersymmetry, and after graduation I'd like to work towards building exciting new technology in the private sector.
